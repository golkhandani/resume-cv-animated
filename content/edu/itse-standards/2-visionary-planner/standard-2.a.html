Here is the assessment plan for students in Grade Twelve in the K-12 system studying Information Technology. According to the plan, one of the modules of the Computer Programming course created and published by the Ministry of Education, Province of British Columbia will be covered.

Arrays, Files, and Searching is a four-week module designed to ensure learners can work with lists and files by the end. Students will be identified based on their prior knowledge, and performance will be regularly assessed using diagnostic, formative, and summative assessments.
