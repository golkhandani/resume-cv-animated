Standard 2.B – Kahoot Workshop

Title: How to use Kahoot! for effective learning! (Teachers’ Walkthrough)

Description: This workshop relates to the ISTE Standards for Educational Leaders, specifically Standard 2.B: Visionary Planner. It empowers teachers to effectively integrate technology, particularly Kahoot!, into their instructional practices.

So What?

The workshop supports educational leaders by equipping them with essential tools to integrate technology in a meaningful and engaging way. By leveraging Kahoot!, teachers foster interactive learning environments that improve retention and student enthusiasm. This aligns with visionary planning for technology-enhanced education.

Now What?

Leaders can apply their workshop insights by using Kahoot! to design interactive quizzes, games, and surveys. Collaboration and ongoing professional development are key to refining the integration of technology into learning. Sharing knowledge among peers promotes a district-wide culture of innovation and engagement.

For more information and to access the Kahoot! course content, visit: Learn Kahoot! – Make Quizzes Fun
